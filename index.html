Welcome to Emmanuel College High School

Emmanuel College High School is a Christian day & boarding school for both boys and girls. We nurture academic excellence, moral values, and spiritual growth in a safe environment.

Mission

To develop well-rounded students with a strong Christian foundation, fostering academic excellence, leadership, and compassion.

Vision

To be a leading Christian educational institution recognized for academic excellence, spiritual growth, and holistic development of every student.
